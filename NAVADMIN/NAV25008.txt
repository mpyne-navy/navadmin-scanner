RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN provides guidance for Fiscal Year 2026 Active Duty 
(AD) Command Master Chief (CMDCM), Master Chief Petty Officer (E9), and 
Senior Chief Petty Officer (E8) selection and screening boards (Cycle 265).

2.  Unless exempted in line with reference (a), eligible candidates, 
including E8 candidates, will participate in the Senior Enlisted Marketplace 
(SEM) screening board.  Exempted candidates, as well as eligible CMDCM 
candidates will participate in enlisted advancement selection boards.

3.  Examination Status Verification Reports will be available on the Navy 
Enlisted Advancement System (NEAS) website https://neas.ncdc.navy.mil/Login
or via MyNavy Portal (MNP) at https://mn3p.navy.mil/
Select "Advancement Profile Sheet - ESO" under "NEAS Resources".

4.  Command Senior Chief (CMDCS) candidates for CMDCM Timeline:
    a.  26 January 2025, 2359 CST.  Letter to the Board (LTB) deadline.
    b.  6 February 2025.  CMDCM selection board convenes.
    c.  7 February 2025.  CMDCM selection board scheduled to adjourn.

5.  E8 and E9 (In rate, both selection and screen) Timeline:
    a.  3 March 2025.  Change of rating deadline.
    b.  3 March 2025.  Chief Petty Officer Leader Development Course
(CPO-LDC) deadline.
    c.  3 March 2025.  Senior Enlisted Academy (SEA) deadline for
E9 candidates.
    d.  20 March 2025, 2359 CST.  LTB deadline.
    e.  31 March 2025.  Selection and Screen boards convene.
    f.  25 April 2025.  Selection and screen boards adjourn.

6.  Eligibility Criteria.  Candidates must meet and maintain the eligibility 
requirements outlined in chapters 2 and 11 of reference
(b) and below, through adjournment of the board:
    a.  The name of the candidate appears on the board eligibility list in 
the correct competitive group on their profile sheet.
Sailor access to profile sheets are available via MNP at 
https://mn3p.navy.mil/
Select "Advancement Profile Sheet - Individual"
under "NEAS Resources".  Candidates should register for e-mail notification 
for profile sheet updates via MNP.  Select "Advancement Profile Sheet - 
Individual email notification" under "NEAS Resources".  Information may 
change on a weekly basis and both commands and Sailors should continue to 
verify.
    b.  Enlisted Leader Development course completion is a required 
prerequisite to be eligible for selection to E8 in line with reference (c).  
E8 candidates must complete the CPO-LDC.  Sailors who have completed the 
course should verify their Electronic Service Record (ESR) has been updated 
with the 8LDC Navy Enlisted Classification (NEC).  If ESR does not reflect 
8LDC NEC, submit certification of completion via LTB.
    c.  Terminal eligibility date: 1 October 2025
    d.  Time-In-Rate dates:
        (1)  For E8:  On or before 1 September 2022.
        (2)  For E9:  On or before 1 July 2022.
    e.  High Year Tenure (HYT).
        (1)  In line with reference (d), HYT Plus remains available to all AD 
paygrades with the exception of AD CMDCMs, AD CMDCSs, and nuclear trained 
Master Chiefs.
        (2) HYT date or approved HYT waivers for AD CMDCMs, AD CMDCSs, and 
nuclear trained Master Chiefs is 1 July 2025 or later.
In line with reference (d), Sailors who otherwise would have reached HYT on 
or after 1 July 2025, and opt to transfer to the Fleet Reserve will be deemed 
a voluntary separation and will not be considered by the selection/screening 
board.
        (3)  Reference (d) should be read in its entirety.
    f.  Sailors with a pending, approved, canceled or disapproved Fleet 
Reserve or retirement request prior to the board convene date will need to 
closely monitor eligibility.  Commands and candidates must request 
revalidation based on request adjudication by sending an e- mail with the 
reason for revalidation to Selection Board Eligibility Branch (PERS-802).
    g.  Security clearance requirements (if required).  Refer to chapter 2 of 
reference (b).  Ratings requiring a security clearance are AC, AE, AG, AO, 
AT, AWF, AWO, AWR, AWS, AWV, AZ, CTI, CTM, CTR, CTT, CWT, EOD, ET, FC, FCA, 
GM, HT, IC, IS, IT, LN, MA, MC, MN, ND, OS, QM, RW, SB, SO, STG, STS, and YN 
as well as all nuclear and submarine ratings.
    h.  Rate specific and competitive group eligibility requirements.  
Candidates must meet special requirements for certain ratings, as outlined in 
paragraph 203 of reference (b).
    i.  Non-nuclear submarine Sailors designated SG or SP as of board 
convening date are not eligible for advancement, in line with reference (e).
    j.  SEA course completion is a required prerequisite to be eligible for 
selection to E9 in line with reference (f).  Sailors who have completed the 
course should verify their ESR has been updated with the 8SEA NEC.  If ESR 
does not reflect 8SEA NEC, submit certificate of completion via LTB.

7.  Competitive Groups.  The below listed ratings are separated into 
competitive groups as applicable to the component of the candidate:
    a.  CTI (E8):  Middle East and North Africa (Group 1), East and Far East 
Asia (Group 2), Latin and South America (Group 3) and Eastern Europe (Group 
4).
    b.  EMN, ETN and MMN:  Submarine (Group 1), Surface (Group 2).
    c.  MU:  Premier Band (Group 1), Fleet Band (Group 2).
    d.  NC:  Career Recruiter (Group 1), Fleet (Group 2).
    e.  ND (E8):  Master Diver (Group 1), First Class Diver (Group

2).

8.  For record review and communication with the boards, refer to reference 
(g).  Board numbers are:  Board 211 (CMDCM advancement board), 210 (E9 SEM 
screen board), 214 (E9 advancement board (SEM exempted ratings)), 235 (E8 SEM 
screen board) and 236 (E8 advancement board (SEM exempted ratings)).  LTBs 
should be submitted via the Electronic Submission of Selection Board 
Documents (ESSBD).
If unable to use ESSBD and for more information on LTBs, visit the following 
link: https://www.mynavyhr.navy.mil/Career-Management/Boards/Active-Duty-
Enlisted/General-Information/
9.  Board results will be posted via BUPERS Online and released by subsequent 
NAVADMIN, with follow-on guidance for the MyNavy Assignments process for 
Sailors screened to compete in the marketplace.  Board selectee profile 
sheets will be published by Naval Education and Training Professional 
Development Center
(NETPDC) via NEAS and MNP websites within 30 days of selection board results 
being released.

10.  Additional information regarding this cycle will be posted on the MyNavy 
HR website at https://www.mynavyhr.navy.mil/Career-Management/Boards/Active-
Duty-Enlisted/
