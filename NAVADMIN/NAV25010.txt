RMKS/1.  This message announces the Fiscal Year (FY) 2024 Deputy Chief of 
Naval Operations for Personnel, Manpower, and Training (OPNAV N1)/Chief of 
Naval Personnel (CNP) Retention Excellence Award Best in Class (REA BIC) 
winners.  The Navy remains committed to retaining talented Sailors to meet 
100% rating fill by 2027.  The engaged leadership of Sailors and Officers at 
every level has successfully created an environment where every member of the 
team is valued and where Sailors and their families continue to choose to 
stay Navy.

2.  In line with reference (a) the following commands are to be applauded for 
their diligence in sustaining the Navy our Nation needs:
       a.  REA BIC Distinction Award.  Recipients of REA BIC were 
competitively compared and considered by their respective Type Commanders.  
All recipients in this category are authorized to fly the Retention 
Excellence Award pennant (blue).
