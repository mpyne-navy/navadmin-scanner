RMKS/1. THIS ORDER TO ACCOUNT WILL COMMENCE AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CLOSEST POINT 
OF APPROACH (CPA) AND APPLIES TO ALL NAVY ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON CIVILIAN 
EMPLOYEES, NAF EMPLOYEES, NEX EMPLOYEES AND APPLICABLE DEPENDENTS WITHIN THE 
ESTABLISHED GEOGRAPHIC AREA OF INTEREST (GAOI) DESIGNATED IN PARAGRAPH 2. 
ECHELON II COMMANDERS AND ALL REGIONAL COMMANDERS SHALL ENSURE WIDEST 
DISSEMINATION TO ALL SUBORDINATE AND TENANT COMMANDS AND DETACHMENTS FOR 
IMMEDIATE ACTION UPON RECEIPT. 
 
2.  THE FOLLOWING GAOI HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED FOR HURRICANE MILTON 2024: 
A.  THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES WITHIN THE STATE OF FLORIDA: BREVARD, HERNANDO, 
HILLSBOROUGH, MANATEE, LAKE, ORANGE, OSCEOLA, PASCO, PINELLAS, POLK, 
SARASOTA, SEMINOLE, SUMTER, VOLUSIA. (ALL DESIGNATED PERSONNEL AND OR 
DEPENDENTS IN THE GAOI NEED TO BE ACCOUNTED FOR). 
 
3.  ALL COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO: 
A.  COMMANDING OFFICERS (CO) AND/OR COMMANDING OFFICER REPRESENTATIVES (COR) 
WILL ENSURE THAT THEIR COMMAND'S NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT 
SYSTEM (NFAAS) DATABASE IS CURRENT AND ACCURATE, TO INCLUDE PHONE NUMBERS AND 
ADDRESSES. ENSURE ANY RECENT CHECK-INS TO YOUR COMMAND WHO HAVE NOT HAD THEIR 
CONTACT INFORMATION UPDATED IN NFAAS, COMMUNICATE RECALL INFORMATION TO 
THEIR RESPECTIVE CHAIN OF COMMAND. 
B.  MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR ALL NAVY ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON CIVILIAN 
EMPLOYEES, NAF EMPLOYEES, NEX EMPLOYEES, AND APPLICABLE DEPENDENTS IN THE 
GAOI. ALL CORS/SPONSORS IN THE AFFECTED GAOI WILL RECEIVE NOTIFICATION FROM 
NFAAS VIA TEXT/EMAIL TO MUSTER THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN NFAAS. COS AND/OR 
CORS WILL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION FOR ACCURATE AND TIMELY ACCOUNTABILITY 
VIA NFAAS. 
IF MEMBERS ARE UNABLE TO ACCESS NFAAS, COS/CORS WILL DETERMINE ACCURATE 
STATUS AND WHEREABOUTS OF THEIR PERSONNEL AND MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR THEM AT 
HOME, WORK, TELEWORK, EVACUATED, OR DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI 
WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/ 
RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER). 
C.  COMMANDS SHALL DIRECT CORS/SPONSORS TO MUSTER IN NFAAS, 
AT https://navyfamily.navy.mil/. IF NFAAS IS NOT AVAILABLE, COMMANDS/MEMBERS 
ARE TO REPORT THE STATUS OF PERSONNEL ACCOUNTABILITY (PA) TO THE AFFECTED 
REGION OPERATIONS CENTER (ROC) OR DIRECT CHAIN OF COMMAND. 
D.  DO NOT DELAY REPORTING ON PERSONNEL UNTIL ALL INFORMATION IS COMPLETE. 
REPORT AND ACCOUNT MUSTERING INFORMATION WHEN OBTAINED, INCLUDING THE POSTING 
OF AMPLIFYING DATA IN THE NOTES AREA ON THE INDIVIDUAL'S ACCOUNTING LINE. 
 
4.  ALL CORS/SPONSORS ARE EXPECTED TO: 
A.  ACCOUNT FOR THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN NFAAS AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA 
AND NOTIFICATION VIA https://navyfamily.navy.mil/cas/login. 
B.  IF UNABLE TO ACCESS NFAAS, SPONSORS SHOULD CONTACT THEIR COR, COMMAND 
MUSTERING OFFICER, REGIONAL COMMANDING OFFICER REPRESENTATIVE, OR SUPERVISOR 
TO CHECK IN (MUSTER), OR MNCC NPC. 
C.  SPONSORS WILL PROVIDE COMMAND REPRESENTATIVES WITH CURRENT RESIDENCE, 
DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, 
TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER). 
D.  FAMILY MEMBERS CAN LOG ON TO NFAAS AT 
https://navyfamily.navy.mil/cas/login AND CHECK IN (MUSTER) ON THE NAVY 
FAMILY MEMBERS SITE. 
 
5. A NEEDS ASSESSMENT HAS BEEN MADE AVAILABLE FOR THOSE INDIVIDUALS IN 
IMPACTED AREAS WHO MAY NEED ASSISTANCE. PLEASE LOG INTO NFAAS AND FILL OUT A 
NEEDS ASSESSMENT TO RECEIVE FLEET AND FAMILY EMERGENCY CASE MANAGER SUPPORT 
WITH YOUR RECOVERY. 
 
6. FOR NFAAS TECHNICAL SUPPORT, CONTACT THE NFAAS HELP DESK AT 
866-946-9183 OR VIA EMAIL AT NFAAS(AT)NAVY.MIL.
